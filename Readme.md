# 📊 Social Media Ads Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes social media advertising data from Facebook and LinkedIn to evaluate campaign performance, audience engagement, lead generation, conversions, and return on ad spend (ROAS) using Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and MySQL.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Optimize advertising spend and maximize ROI  
- Improve campaign performance and conversion rates  
- Identify high-performing audiences and platforms  
- Enhance engagement and lead generation strategies  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel  
- Power BI  
- Tableau  
- MySQL  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset includes advertising and post-level data from Facebook and LinkedIn such as campaign details, impressions, reach, clicks, engagement metrics, leads, purchases, audience demographics, and platform information.

### Facebook Dataset Highlights
- Campaign Name, Ad Set, Ad Name  
- Impressions, Reach, Link Clicks  
- Cost, Website Leads, Website Purchases  
- Purchase Value, Age Group, Gender, Device Type, Region  

### LinkedIn Dataset Highlights
- Post ID, Post Type, Topic  
- Views, Reactions, Comments, Reposts  
- Engagement Rate  
- Day of Week, Time of Day, Target Industry, Seniority  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing and inconsistent values  
- Standardized date formats  
- Created calculated fields for CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS, and Engagement Rate  
- Validated metrics using Excel and SQL  

---

## 🧩 Data Modeling
- Designed fact and dimension tables  
- Built relationships between campaigns, audience, and performance data  
- Used SQL for aggregation and KPI validation  
- Connected SQL database to Power BI and Tableau  

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

### Facebook KPIs
- Total Ad Spend  
- Total Impressions & Reach  
- Total Link Clicks  
- Click-Through Rate (CTR %)  
- Conversion Rate (%)  
- Cost per Purchase (CPP)  
- Website Leads  
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)  
- Social Interactions  

### LinkedIn KPIs
- Total Posts  
- Total Views  
- Total Reactions, Comments, Reposts  
- Engagement Rate (%)  
- Most Engaging Post  
- Best Day & Time to Post  
- Post Performance by Topic

## 🔍 Key Insights
- High CTR campaigns do not always result in high conversions, indicating landing page or targeting issues  
- Mobile devices contribute the majority of clicks but may have lower conversion rates  
- Specific audience segments (age group & region) generate higher engagement and leads  
- LinkedIn posts perform best on specific days and time slots depending on content type  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Reallocate budget toward high-ROAS campaigns  
- Optimize landing pages to improve conversion rates  
- Focus on high-performing audience segments  
- Schedule LinkedIn posts during peak engagement times  
- Improve ad creatives for low-performing campaigns
